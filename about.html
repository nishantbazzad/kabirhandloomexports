Our Story

Kabir International is a leading home textiles manufacturing unit based in India. We specialize in designing and developing high-quality Bathmats, Rugs , Cushion Cover , Placemats , Table Runner , Carpets Baskets , Throws & other home textiles products that have been exported worldwide since 2021.
